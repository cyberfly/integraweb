Common questions

Before we get started.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?+

Not at all. We handle all the technical work. Your job is to tell us about your business, your goals, and what's slowing you down — and we take it from there.

How long does an engagement typically take?+

It depends on the scope. A focused automation project can be live in 2–4 weeks. A full AI integration with team training might run 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline after the free consultation.

Will AI automation replace our employees?+

No — and that's not our goal. We automate the tedious, repetitive work so your team can focus on the high-value tasks that require human judgment, creativity, and relationships. AI handles the busywork; your people handle the meaningful work.

Which tools and platforms do you work with?+

We're platform-agnostic and work with the tools you already use — HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Notion, Google Workspace, and many more. If you're unsure what tools are right for you, we'll help you evaluate options as part of the engagement.

What does the free consultation cover?+

A free 30-minute call where we review your current workflows, identify your biggest AI opportunities, and recommend concrete first steps. No pitch, no pressure — just a useful conversation about your business.
