You don't need to learn to code to build software anymore. You need to learn how to **brief an AI** that codes for you — and that's exactly what this full-day workshop teaches.

This **second edition** is tighter and more practical than the first: less talk, more building. Over the course of one Saturday, you'll build **three complete projects from scratch** — a live personal website, an installable app, and a full sales platform with login, an admin panel, product management, and payments. You won't write the code yourself. Instead, you'll learn agentic briefing — describing what you want clearly enough that Claude builds it for you.

This is for people with ideas and no technical background: business owners, marketers, founders, and absolute beginners. By the end of the day your projects will be live on the internet — and you'll have the prompting skills, plus free templates and presets, to keep building long after the workshop ends.
